    Bash - Date and Timestamps

    Print current date

    date

    Wed Aug  5 01:01:41 UTC 2018

    Print current timestamp

    date +%s

    1549024740

    Convert timestamp to date:

    date -d @1549024740

    Results: Fri Feb  1 12:39:00 UTC 2019
